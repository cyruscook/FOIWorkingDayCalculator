Response due date for a 20 working day limit:

You should receive a response by
midnight on the 23 March 2023

There are some situations where the authority may be able to respond later than this:

  • If the authority needs more time to consider a public interest test, they may delay by a reasonable time (normally no more than 20 working days), but they should notify you that they are doing this
  • If the authority is a school, in which case they must respond within 20 working school days or 60 working days (whichever is shorter)
  • Depending on the information requested, if the authority is an archival authority they may have a 30 working day limit
  • If the information requested is held by someone involved in a military operation or the preperation of a military operation there may be an up to 60 working day limit
  • If the information requested is held overseas there may be an up to 60 working day limit
